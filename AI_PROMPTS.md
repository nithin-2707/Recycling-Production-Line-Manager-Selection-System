# AI Evaluation Prompts for Recycling Production Line Manager

## Overview
These prompts are designed to evaluate candidates across three critical dimensions: Crisis Management, Sustainability Knowledge, and Team Motivation. Each prompt includes a scoring rubric to ensure consistent, objective evaluation.

---

## Prompt 1: Crisis Management Evaluation

### Context
You are evaluating a candidate for a Recycling Production Line Manager position. This role requires handling equipment failures, safety incidents, supply disruptions, and operational emergencies while maintaining production continuity and team safety.

### Prompt
```
Evaluate this candidate's crisis management capabilities based on the following information:

**Candidate Profile:**
- Name: {candidate_name}
- Experience: {years_experience} years
- Current Role: {current_role}
- Relevant Skills: {skills}
- Certifications: {certifications}

**Evaluation Scenario:**
A critical sorting machine has failed during peak operations, causing a complete production line stoppage. Contaminated materials are backing up, workers are standing idle, and the facility is at risk of missing crucial client deadlines. Additionally, there's a minor chemical spill in the adjacent area requiring immediate attention.

**Assessment Questions:**
1. How would this candidate likely prioritize immediate actions?
2. What systems or protocols might they implement to prevent similar crises?
3. How effectively could they communicate with stakeholders during the crisis?
4. What decision-making approach would they likely employ under pressure?

**Scoring Criteria:**
Rate the candidate on a scale of 0-100 based on:
- Immediate response capability (25 points)
- Safety prioritization (25 points)
- Communication effectiveness (20 points)
- Long-term prevention thinking (15 points)
- Resource allocation under pressure (15 points)

Provide a numerical score and brief justification.
```

### Evaluation Rubric

| Score Range | Assessment | Characteristics |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| 90-100 | Exceptional | Demonstrates comprehensive crisis protocols, exemplary safety focus, clear communication chains, proactive prevention strategies |
| 80-89 | Strong | Shows solid emergency response skills, good safety awareness, effective stakeholder communication, recovery planning |
| 70-79 | Competent | Adequate crisis handling, standard safety measures, basic communication, some prevention awareness |
| 60-69 | Developing | Limited crisis experience, reactive approach, communication gaps, minimal prevention focus |
| Below 60 | Insufficient | Lacks crisis management framework, poor prioritization, inadequate safety focus |

## Prompt 2: Sustainability Knowledge Evaluation

### Context
The recycling industry is at the forefront of environmental sustainability. This role requires deep understanding of circular economy principles, waste stream management, environmental regulations, and sustainable operations practices.

### Prompt
```
Assess this candidate's sustainability knowledge and application capabilities:

**Candidate Profile:**
- Name: {candidate_name}
- Education: {education}
- Experience: {years_experience} years in {current_role}
- Certifications: {certifications}
- Skills: {skills}

**Evaluation Focus Areas:**

1. **Circular Economy Understanding:**
   - Can they articulate how recycling fits into larger sustainability goals?
   - Understanding of material lifecycle and recovery optimization

2. **Environmental Compliance:**
   - Knowledge of EPA regulations and local environmental laws
   - Experience with environmental management systems (ISO 14001, etc.)

3. **Waste Stream Optimization:**
   - Contamination reduction strategies
   - Material recovery rate improvement
   - Quality control for recycled outputs

4. **Sustainable Operations:**
   - Energy efficiency in recycling operations
   - Water usage and waste minimization
   - Carbon footprint reduction initiatives

5. **Innovation & Adaptation:**
   - Awareness of emerging recycling technologies
   - Commitment to continuous improvement in sustainability metrics

**Scoring Criteria:**
Rate on a scale of 0-100:
- Regulatory knowledge (20 points)
- Circular economy comprehension (25 points)
- Waste optimization expertise (25 points)
- Sustainable operations practices (20 points)
- Innovation mindset (10 points)

Provide score with supporting evidence from candidate's background.
```

### Evaluation Rubric

| Score Range | Assessment | Characteristics |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| 90-100 | Expert | Comprehensive sustainability expertise, proven track record, certifications, innovative approaches, regulatory mastery |
| 80-89 | Advanced | Strong environmental knowledge, relevant certifications, demonstrated results, good regulatory understanding |
| 70-79 | Proficient | Solid sustainability foundation, basic certifications, standard practices, adequate compliance knowledge |
| 60-69 | Basic | Limited sustainability exposure, minimal certifications, learning phase, basic compliance awareness |
| Below 60 | Inadequate | Insufficient environmental knowledge, no relevant certifications, minimal sustainability focus |

## Prompt 3: Team Motivation & Leadership Evaluation

### Context
A Recycling Production Line Manager must inspire and lead diverse teams in challenging conditions. The role requires building cohesive teams, maintaining high morale during demanding shifts, developing staff capabilities, and fostering a culture of safety and excellence.

### Prompt
```
Evaluate this candidate's team motivation and leadership potential:

**Candidate Profile:**
- Name: {candidate_name}
- Current Role: {current_role}
- Experience: {years_experience} years
- Skills: {skills}
- Leadership Background: {relevant_experience}

**Assessment Dimensions:**

1. **Leadership Style:**
   - Analyze their likely approach to team management
   - Ability to adapt leadership to diverse team members
   - Balance between authority and empowerment

2. **Motivational Capabilities:**
   - Strategies for maintaining team engagement in repetitive work
   - Recognition and reward system implementation
   - Building pride in environmental mission

3. **Communication:**
   - Clarity in instructions and expectations
   - Active listening and feedback incorporation
   - Conflict resolution approaches

4. **Team Development:**
   - Commitment to training and skill development
   - Succession planning and career path creation
   - Performance management effectiveness

5. **Cultural Building:**
   - Fostering safety-first culture
   - Promoting collaboration and mutual support
   - Managing multi-shift team dynamics

**Evaluation Scenario:**
The team morale is low due to increased production targets, recent safety incidents causing stress, and turnover in key positions. How would this candidate rebuild trust, re-engage the team, and restore performance while maintaining safety standards?

**Scoring Criteria:**
Rate on a scale of 0-100:
- Leadership effectiveness (25 points)
- Motivational strategy (25 points)
- Communication skills (20 points)
- Development focus (15 points)
- Culture building (15 points)

Provide numerical score and key supporting points.
```

### Evaluation Rubric

| Score Range | Assessment | Characteristics |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| 90-100 | Inspirational Leader | Transformational leadership, proven team development, exceptional communication, strong culture builder |
| 80-89 | Effective Leader | Solid leadership skills, good motivational strategies, clear communication, team development focus |
| 70-79 | Capable Leader | Competent team management, standard motivational approaches, adequate communication |
| 60-69 | Emerging Leader | Basic leadership skills, limited motivational toolkit, developing communication abilities |
| Below 60 | Insufficient | Weak leadership capabilities, poor motivational skills, communication challenges |

---

## Scoring Methodology

### Overall Candidate Ranking Formula

```
Overall Score = (Crisis Management × 0.35) + (Sustainability × 0.35) + (Team Motivation × 0.30)
```

**Weighting Justification:**
- **Crisis Management (35%):** Critical for operational continuity and safety
- **Sustainability (35%):** Core mission alignment and regulatory compliance
- **Team Motivation (30%):** Essential for long-term operational excellence

### Final Rating Categories

| Overall Score | Category | Hiring Recommendation |
|---------------|----------|----------------------|
| 90-100 | Exceptional Candidate | Strong Hire - Fast Track |
| 85-89 | Excellent Candidate | Hire - Priority Interview |
| 80-84 | Very Good Candidate | Hire - Standard Process |
| 75-79 | Good Candidate | Consider - Further Assessment |
| 70-74 | Acceptable Candidate | Consider - With Development Plan |
| Below 70 | Not Recommended | Do Not Proceed |

## Implementation Notes

### For AI Evaluation Systems:
1. **Input Preparation:** Extract structured data from candidate resumes/profiles
2. **Batch Processing:** Evaluate candidates in batches for consistency
3. **Cross-Validation:** Compare AI scores with human expert ratings periodically
4. **Bias Monitoring:** Regular audits to ensure fair evaluation across demographics
5. **Transparency:** Provide candidates with scoring breakdown upon request

### For Human Reviewers:
- Use prompts as structured interview guides
- Score independently before discussion
- Document specific evidence for each score
- Calibrate scoring across multiple reviewers
- Combine AI insights with human judgment for final decisions

## API Integration Example (Claude AI)

```javascript
async function evaluateCandidate(candidate, evaluationType) {
  const prompts = {
    crisis: CRISIS_MANAGEMENT_PROMPT,
    sustainability: SUSTAINABILITY_PROMPT,
    teamMotivation: TEAM_MOTIVATION_PROMPT
  };
  
  const prompt = prompts[evaluationType]
    .replace('{candidate_name}', candidate.name)
    .replace('{years_experience}', candidate.experience)
    .replace('{current_role}', candidate.currentRole)
    .replace('{skills}', candidate.skills.join(', '))
    .replace('{certifications}', candidate.certifications.join(', '))
    .replace('{education}', candidate.education);
  
  const response = await fetch('https://api.anthropic.com/v1/messages', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Content-Type': 'application/json',
      'x-api-key': process.env.ANTHROPIC_API_KEY,
      'anthropic-version': '2023-06-01'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      model: 'claude-3-sonnet-20240229',
      max_tokens: 1000,
      messages: [{
        role: 'user',
        content: prompt
      }]
    })
  });
  
  const data = await response.json();
  // Parse score from response
  return extractScore(data.content[0].text);
}
```
